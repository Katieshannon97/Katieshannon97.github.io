about

The notion of making jewelry began when I would pick out an outfit and wish I had a set of jewelry like artwork to go with it. Very few times would I have a necklace and earring set that matched. I would pull out a dress or blouse or sweater and say do I have something that matches this shade of blue, red, turquoise?

Even more so to me jewelry was art. My glasses were a piece of jewelry and like necklaces and earrings a statement piece. I would wear my creations and people often would ask where I got them. After explaining I made them I was receiving requests, but in a unique niche... custom jewelry to go with that power suit or gown or sweater or summer, spring, fall or winter outfit. I decided to start a business making unique pieces. One of a kind pieces that I would never duplicate. I love old movies and music and how it makes a girl feel like a lady. Women dressed up to go out and showed grace and class. So many trinkets, crystals and gems to choose from was the notion. Each piece incorporates crystals for a touch of glam.
